Chapter Activities

Enterprise Fall Festival

At the Enterprise Fall Festival our chapter sold 3D printed Boll Weevil Monuments, ornaments engraved with the Boll Weevil monument, and several CNCed door hangers. While fundraising, our chapter drove a VEX competition bot and talked about what TSA is and what we do as a chapter.

Blood Drive and Escape Room

Our chapter donated blood through the LifeSouth Bloodmobile on February 11th. Immediately following the bllod drive, we participated in an escape room entitled "The Shack" at Escape Enterprise.
